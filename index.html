Project Proposal: Wildfire Reporter

The Problem

Wildfires are a growing threat to the safety of people and properties alike, all across the United States, and especially in western states like Washington, Oregon, and California. While Central and Eastern Washington have always struggled with fires, they've began to spread farther West as we see from fires like the Bolt Creek fire in 2022. With climate we can expect to see these fires getting more and more common in areas less and less prepared to deal with it. One of the first steps to keeping up with the increasing wildfire threat is being prepared, and that's something we aim to help with.

Our Solution

We plan to make a web application that helps keep people aware of fires near them, evacuation levels, and gives advice on what action to take based on proximity to a fire. The app will also allow users to report fire incidents near now, or suspected fire incidets.

Functionality

  • User will be able to register for an account, to track their chosen address for fire alerts
  • User will be able to post suspected fire sitings on the site, or certain users (maybe admin?) will be able to post official fire updates on the site. Fire alert messages will be sent to people with addresses logged near the location
  • Users will have access to a variety of fire / evacuation advice, with certain advice reccomended at closer proximity to fire.

Conclusion

By making users more aware of fire danger through alerts (not using javascript alerts, don't worry), enabling quick official reporting of potential fires, and presenting up-to-date fire safety information we hope to make fire season safer for everyone in Washington State
